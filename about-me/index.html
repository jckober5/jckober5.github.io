A little about me...

My name is Joshua Kober a Data Analyst with Penn Foster Group, an organization that provides accredited online community college education. I am an individual with a passion for learning. I graduated from Southern New Hampshire University with a Masters Degree in Data Analytics in 2017 and have since been helping organizations understand and visualize their data effectively. I have always had the heart to serve and in this role I have done just that.

I have experience in languages such as SQL, R, and Python. I also have used and managed visualization softwares/tools such as Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. More recently I have been a Tableau Administrator handling user creation and permissions for both inside and outside the organization. I've always been one that has pursued learning new skills that can help those around me.

Outside of work and study I like to spend my time with my family. As a family we love to go up the Mountains here in Utah and see the beautiful area around us. When I'm not with my family I like to spend time either playing tennis, basketball, or relaxing outside.
