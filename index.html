Project: Temples across the World

Leveraging Apache Airflow, I've developed a sophisticated web scraping pipeline to extract real-time data on the status of temples for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This automated system enables me to efficiently gather and process information, allowing for dynamic visualization of temples categorized by their current state: under construction, announced, or operational. The resulting insights provide a comprehensive overview of the Church's global temple network, facilitating informed analysis and strategic planning within Tableau.

Project: Youtube Analytics

By leveraging the YouTube Analytics API and Apache Airflow, I've developed a sophisticated data pipeline that enables comprehensive analysis of artists' YouTube performance. This automated system allows me to extract and compare key metrics such as channel growth, video performance, and audience engagement across multiple artists. Through detailed analytics, I can track real-time insights including total views, subscriber growth, and the trajectory of their most successful music videos, providing a nuanced understanding of their digital success and content strategy.

Project: Data Job Postings

Leveraging the Google API and Apache Airflow, I've developed a sophisticated data pipeline to extract and analyze job postings within the data industry. This automated system enables me to gather real-time insights into market demands, required skill sets, and compensation trends across various data-related roles. By processing this information, I can provide a comprehensive overview of the current job market landscape, facilitating informed career decisions and identifying emerging trends in the data field within Tableau.
