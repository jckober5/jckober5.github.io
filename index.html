About Me

I am Josh, an excited Data Analyst. I have been trained in understanding and organizing data in ways that bring new and helpful insights to different organizations everywhere. I have experience utilizing many tools to organize, analyze, and visualize data. These include R, Python, Excel, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Snowflake, Power BI, and Tableau. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Statistics from Brigham Young University and a Master's Degree in Data Analysis from Southern New Hampshire University. I have worked over 5 years as a Data Analyst cleaning, organizing, analyzing, and visualizing data. I have worked with several different companies in helping them understand their data needs.
